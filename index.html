What This Program Is Not

  • This is not a quick “life coach” certification or a business program.
  • It’s not business coaching, social media coaching or how to make money online.
  • It’s not therapy, nor is it a substitute for mental health treatment.

  • It’s not passive learning — it’s a rigorous, hands-on certification that requires full participation, practice, and demonstration of skill.

Please note graduates of this program are not “employees” of Science of People, but licensed practitioners who meet our standards and represent our methods ethically in their own coaching, training, or organizational work.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to complete the program?

The certification is designed to be completed in 3 months, but it’s self-paced — you can move faster or slower depending on your schedule. We will have live teaching sessions during the 3 months, so we encourage you to watch each module before the live sessions so we can answer your questions.

What’s the time commitment each week?

Expect to spend about 3–5 hours per week engaging with the modules, practicing, and reflecting. Because it’s self-paced, you can structure it around your life and work.

Are there pre-requisites?

There are no pre-requisites, however Vanessa’s book Cues: The Secret Language of Charismatic Communication is required reading in the training and will be used extensively. The more you are familiar with the material the better, but absolutely not required ahead of time.

Do I need to be an experienced coach to apply?

No — but we are looking for folks with relevant experience. This program is for people who already lead, guide, or teach others in some capacity: managers, HR leaders, coaches, therapists, teachers, facilitators, and organizational trainers. If you’re already helping people, this certification will give you the frameworks to help them more effectively.

Can I use this certification in my existing coaching or therapy practice?

Yes! That is our fondest wish. These tools are built to be flexible and can be applied across fields — from corporate training and leadership coaching to relationship coaching and therapy. Everyone needs communication, and you’ll have a framework that adapts to your specialty.

Can I use this certification inside my company?

Absolutely. Many applicants are intrapreneurs — leaders inside organizations who want to run trainings, lead workshops, or be the “in-house expert” on communication. This certification gives you the credibility and content to do exactly that. Let us know if your company would like to buy a license for multiple trainers.

Is there a payment plan available?

Yes. We offer a monthly payment plan in addition to the one-time tuition. You’ll see the options during the application process.

Why is there a $500 annual renewal?

The renewal ensures your certification stays current and respected. It covers updated materials and research, access to quarterly live office hours, membership in the private community, and recertification checks to uphold standards. This way, when people see you hold this credential, they know it represents ongoing mastery, not a one-time course.

Will this guarantee me clients or higher income?

We cannot promise revenue — but we designed this program to give you tools you can immediately apply to grow your practice, charge higher rates, and lead trainings. Most importantly, it elevates your credibility and authority. What you do with that will be up to you — but we believe it will give you a powerful edge.

Can I customize the curriculum?

Yes! Our materials are designed to fit your branding, flavor and perspective if you wish. Our hope is for you to take our curriculum and fit it into your existing practice or teaching.
